Integrations

Connect external services to your speaker.

Spotify ›
Per-household-member OAuth — link each person's Spotify account so voice commands route music to the right person (e.g. "play Brittany's Daily Mix"). Default account handles bare requests.
Google ›
Calendar and Gmail. Per-household-member OAuth — link each person's Google account so voice queries route to the right inbox and calendar (e.g. "what's on Brittany's calendar?"). Read-only access; the speaker never sends or modifies.
More integrations coming. Today's cut covers Spotify (the music streamer the household actually uses) and Google (Calendar and email — the queries that come up daily). Future candidates depend on what's actually useful day-to-day.
