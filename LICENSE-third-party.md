# Third-party Licenses and Notices

This is the first-pass JTS attribution inventory. It covers
third-party code, generated assets, model/data files, and important
install-time or firmware components that are checked into this repo,
downloaded by JTS scripts, or likely to matter when redistributing a
Pi image or firmware build.

Last reviewed: 2026-05-26.

This is not yet a full SBOM. Package-manager transitive dependencies
still need automated inventory before a public binary/image release.
When a row says "needs verification", do not treat the component as
cleared for redistribution until the upstream license file and any
required notices have been checked into this repo or replaced by a
permissively licensed alternative.
